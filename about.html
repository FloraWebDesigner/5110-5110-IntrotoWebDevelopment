Pet Love House was founded in 2030 by Flora, a researcher specializing in pet therapy.The idea was inspired by animal shelters in the post epidemic era.

As COVID-19 came to an end, more and more people abandoned their pets as they went back to work in their offices, and most of them happened to adopt pets during the epidemic due to the loneliness of social isolation. As a result, they are likely to lack a sense of responsibility in caring for their pets, and this is certainly a wake-up call to the community that people should not take pet adoption lightly.

Pet Love House provides pet owners with a place to host their pets on a short to medium term basis, giving them room to think without having to make a quick decision to abandon their pet, as well as instantly relieving them from the stress of not having time to care for their pets.
